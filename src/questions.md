# Questions

* What is ... ? - ... là gì?
* Which ... ? - ... nào?
* Who - Ai
* If ... - Nếu ...
* Have you [verb] yet? - Subject + Verb + **chưa?**

  > Anh ăn **chưa?** — Have you eaten yet?
  > Bạn đến **chưa?** — Have you arrived yet?

  Answer: Verb + **rồi** (yes, already) or **chưa** (no, not yet).
