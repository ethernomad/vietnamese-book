## Already / Not Yet

### Already — rồi

**rồi** is placed at the **end** of a sentence to indicate an action has been completed.

* Subject + **đã** + Verb + **rồi** — already [verb] (formal)
* Subject + Verb + **rồi** — already [verb] (casual)

Examples:
* Tôi đã ăn rồi. — I already ate.
* Tôi đến rồi. — I've arrived (already).
* Tôi hiểu rồi. — I understand now / I get it.

### Not Yet — chưa

**chưa** is placed **before the verb** to mean "not yet".

* Subject + **chưa** + Verb — not yet [verb]

Examples:
* Tôi chưa ăn. — I haven't eaten yet.
* Tôi chưa đến. — I haven't arrived yet.
* Tôi chưa hiểu. — I don't understand yet.

### Question Form — Verb + chưa?

To ask "have you [verb] yet?", put **chưa** at the **end** of the question.

* Subject + Verb + (Object) + **chưa?**

The answer uses **rồi** (yes) or **chưa** (no).

Examples:
* **Q:** Anh ăn chưa? — Have you eaten yet?
  **A:** Ăn rồi. — Ate already.
  **A:** Chưa. — Not yet.
* **Q:** Bạn đến chưa? — Have you arrived yet?
  **A:** Đến rồi. — Arrived already.
  **A:** Chưa. — Not yet.
