# Pronouns

* I - tôi
* friend - bạn
* they - họ
* everybody - mọi người
* you, someone of similar age, a classmate, or a stranger (polite/neutral) - bạn
* everybody - mọi người

## Mình

Usage             Meaning	     Context
First Person	    I / Me	     Friendly, close (friends, peers).
Reflexive	        -self	       Doing something to oneself.
Second Person	    You / Honey  Married couples or deep lovers calling each other.
Inclusive Plural	We / Us	     "You and I" together.
